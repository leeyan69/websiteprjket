About Travis Scott

Travis Scott, originally Jacques Berman Webster II, is an American rapper, singer, and producer. Born on April 30, 1992, in Houston, Texas, he gained recognition for his unique style blending hip hop, rock, and electronic music influences.

Scott's latest album, "Utopia", released in 2023, showcases his evolution as an artist, featuring collaborations with top musicians and exploring themes of fame, ambition, and personal growth.
